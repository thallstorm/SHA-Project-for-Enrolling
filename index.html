Yannis and Stella's Apartment

Enjoy your holiday in a cycladic house, 3 minutes from the beach, in a small village of Naxos

Our Apartment has everything you may need for your Summer vacation! It has a fully equipped kitchen, 2 rooms, a bathroom with a bathtub, 2 balconies, 3 beds and 1 couch.

Aformoi or wild beach! Reach it in a couple of minutes from Yannis and Stella's home!

If you are looking for a quiet place, close to the beach, that gives you access to the rest of the island as well, we are waiting for you in Apollon!
